Project plan

Title

Paw Prints

Goal

This website will introduce people to the doggy daycare and boarding business, wether they have an interest in getting a job there or simply bringing their dog to a kennel like the one I work at. It will also give information about different breeds I have worked with and what some of my favorites are.

Target Audience

The target audience for my website is anyone who is interested in the doggy daycare and boarding business. It is also intended for any dog lovers as they will be able to view pictures of many different breeds and to get a little insight on what I have been doing for the past three years as a dog walker.

Main Features

Web site with links to information, pictures, and stories about my experiences working at the doggy daycare. A gallery of images of different dogs I have worked with around different times of the year.

Personality

My site will have a warm color scheme to make new visitors to the site feel more invited and calm. It is not a professional website or used for business so it will display more of a social conscience, and it will change over time as more pictures can be added and I will be able to add onto the blog as I have more experiences to talk about. I would like the person to feel inclined to return to the website after viewing it, and to feel like they got some insight on the doggy daycare and boarding business.

Limiting Factors

One of my limiting factors is my inexperience with Javascript, I will need to look more into the integration of it with webpages. Time is another constraint as I don't have an endless amount to work on this project. I will be working with photos I have taken so I am hoping there will be enough.

Development guide

Site structure

  • Root Directory
    • images directory – contains jpgs and gifs
    • styles directory – contains all of the style sheets
    • index.html – home page for site, site purpose and overview
    • photos.html - gallery of images of different dogs that go to my work.
    • Favorites.html - gallery of images of my favorite dogs at work and information about them and the corresponding breed that they are.
    • blog.html - information about when I started working at the doggy daycare and different stories about my experiences.
    • about.html - general information about pricing and how to get started/prepared for taking your dog to daycare/boarding.
